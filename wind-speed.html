Wind speed vs latitude

From the scatterplot, we can visually tell that there are more data points on the Northern hemisphere and speed appear to increase as you go higher. There is a positive correlation between wind speeds and latitude in the Northern hemisphere.
Southern hemisphere wind speeds tend to reduce as you approach the equator hence giving a negative correlation in the Southern hemisphere.
